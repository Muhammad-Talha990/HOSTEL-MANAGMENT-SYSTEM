Our Facilities


Crystal Home offers a range of facilities designed to enhance your stay


  • WiFi Enjoy high-speed internet access throughout our premises, powered by a router that supports WPA2 Wi-Fi encryption keys for secure browsing.

  • Laundry Our Laundry Now service detects whether washers or dryers are in operation, ensuring efficient use of resources.
    After completing a cycle, the machine's status changes to 'Available' after five minutes, allowing guests to easily see availability.

  • Transport We offer convenient transportation options to and from the airport, city center, and other key locations,
    ensuring a seamless travel experience for our guests.
